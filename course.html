Our Courses

Courses We Offer

If you want to teach people a new way of thinking, don’t bother trying to teach them. Instead, give them a tool, the use of which will lead to new ways of thinking.

Computer Science & Engineering

The Computer Science Engineering is a proficient branch, that offers technical and professional competency to students and also aids in inculcating moral and ethical values. The world is evolving technologically and Computer Science is the discipline that lies at the intersection of computing mathematics & applications in science, engineering and social sciences. We develop advances in computing that enable discovery & innovation in other domains.

Electronics & Communication Engineering

The Department of ECE endeavors to continue its accomplishment from the year 2011 for Under Graduation course (B.E.) with initial intake of 60. With the remarkable growth the intake augmented to 120 in the year 2013 and to 180 from the year 2018. It is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation since 2017 for its excellence. Also it is affiliated to Anna University. The Department provides comprehensive knowledge to design, develop and investigate the various Electronic equipment’s and Communication Systems. The state of art Laboratory facilities enable the student’s with practical knowledge and paves way for research.

Mechanical Engineering

Our institute/college offers multiple events to express their talents, knowledge and other skills. Our Facilities

St. Joseph's Library

The library is fully automated and digitalised. The staff and students visit the library as often as possible for reference and for research. The poor student-teachers avail the facility of book bank to borrow books.

St. Joseph's Sports Facility

Sports teaches you character, it teaches you to play by the rules, it teaches you to know what it feels like to win and to lose, it teaches you about life. Our college offers exclusive care to players involved in various sports activities and it takes every step to ensure that sports are given equal importance as academics. It is an undeniable fact that our college was the pioneer to start sports quota admissions in the state and this was followed by all the other reputed institutions. And now, were have the highest intake of sports students.

St. Joseph's Mess Hall

The mess hall covers a total area of 70,000 Sq. Ft with separate Vegetarian and Non-Vegetarian kitchens that come up with mouth-watering meals three times a day, all seven days of the week. The tantalising aroma drifting from the kitchens is guaranteed to replace any other coherent thought one might possess with the idea of fine dining. Healthy eating has never looked more appetizing. All food is cooked by extremely talented chefs with a passion for preparing mouth-watering dishes.
